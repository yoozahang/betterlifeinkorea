About Project : Better Life in Korea

This site is a beta service for foreigners interested in Korea.
We aim for a "Better Life in Korea for Foreigners" by resolving language difficulties and information gaps.

By helping foreigners' better lives in Korea,
We will solve the problem of reducing the labor force in Korea.
We aim to improve Korea's competitiveness in the global market through the inflow of foreign talent.
